About Nek Chand Towers

Nek Chand Towers (NCT-1 to NCT-6) are the official Boys Hostels at Chandigarh University, situated on the backside of the campus in Rupnagar, Mohali, Punjab. Each tower provides 4-seater rooms with facilities like mess, gym, laundry, and canteens. NCT-6 is specially reserved for international students.

The Hostel Director, Mr. Ashwani, sits in NCT-4 and oversees hostel-related operations. Use the chat below for quick information or assistance.
